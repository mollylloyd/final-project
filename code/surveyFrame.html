Japan

Triple Disaster Survey

From April 20 to May 1 2014, we conducted a survey among Japanese who use Facebook. We asked them to answer questions and share their thoughts and feelings about the disaster. We received over 300 survey responses. Each dot represents a response from our survey. Mouse over any dot to reveal that person's thoughts or click down to see more results from our survey.
